About FMTRI

The Future Military Technology Research Institution is a conceptual research organization dedicated to studying emerging defense technologies through simulation, ethics, and foresight — never weapon creation.

Research Areas

Autonomous & AI Systems

Studying human–AI collaboration risks and decision support systems.

Emerging Threat Modeling

Simulating hypothetical future risks before they reach reality.

Cyber & Infrastructure Defense

Protecting civilian systems from non-kinetic threats.

Ethics & Oversight

Ensuring transparency, restraint, and civilian safety.

Mission Statement

To safeguard humanity through ethical analysis, simulation, and foresight — preparing for future threats without contributing to harm.
